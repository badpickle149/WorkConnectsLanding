Welcome to WorkConnects!

Mid-large tech companies can have thousands of employees. With so many people, it can be intimidating to make new friends, especially when you have to work around your schedule. At WorkConnects, we'll do all the hard work for you! Get automatically matched with your peers and start meeting new people now! Click here to make a profile.

Make an account
When you register, give us basic information like where you work, job role, age, schedule, etc. We'll use this information to get you the best matches.

Let us do the hard work
A couple times a week, we'll match you with someone new. These matches will be specially curated to you based on the information you provided us. Just sit back and relax, we'll do all the work for you!

Get in touch
After connecting with someone, send them a message to get to know them. Better yet - meet up with them! We'll only match you if you both have overlapping free time.
